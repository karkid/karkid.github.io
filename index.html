About

Hi I'm Dheeraj. I love good design and creative community building. I'm believe in "Good Design Is Good Business" :-)

Right now, I help people make awesome things happen with Onshape (Client of Graebert Gmbh), a versatile cloud based CAD software that can be used to increase the productivity of the designer, improve the quality of design, improve communications through documentation, and to create a database for manufacturing. Prior to joining the Onshape's team, I was a Software engineer at Sevya Multimedia, a destination for consulting casino services.

Through Sevya I worked as a consultant on multiple firms like STMicroelectronics, Aristocrat etc. as well as I also worked on the internal project "SurveyGamez", a platform that provides a powerful question builder that is extensive in the question types it covers and provides you a lot of opportunities to be creative.

I'm open to any opportunity and challenges for Good Design.
