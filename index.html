About

Hi I'm Dheeraj. I love good design and creative community building. I'm believe in "Good Design Is Good Business" :-)

Right now, I help people make awesome things happen with Onshape (Client of Graebert Gmbh), a versatile cloud based CAD software that can be used to increase the productivity of the designer, improve the quality of design, improve communications through documentation, and to create a database for manufacturing. I'm open to any opportunity and challenges for Good Design.
